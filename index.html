Menstrual Health

Symptoms of Imbalance

  • Irregular periods or spotting
  • Severe cramps (Dysmenorrhea)
  • Extreme mood swings (PMDD/PMS)
  • Heavy bleeding or very light flow

Common Causes

  • Chronic stress and poor sleep
  • Hormonal fluctuations (Estrogen/Progesterone)
  • Nutrient deficiencies (Iron, Magnesium)

Thyroid Function

Symptoms of Imbalance

  • Constant fatigue and lethargy
  • Unexplained weight changes (gain or loss)
  • Brain fog and difficulty concentrating
  • Feeling cold constantly (Hypothyroid)

Common Causes

  • Autoimmune conditions (Hashimoto's, Grave's)
  • Nutrient deficiencies (Iodine, Selenium, Zinc)
  • Chronic stress elevating cortisol

PCOS & PCOD

Symptoms of Imbalance

  • Irregular or missed cycles
  • Unwanted hair growth (Hirsutism)
  • Stubborn acne and weight gain
  • Difficulty conceiving

Common Causes

  • Insulin resistance (most common driver)
  • Elevated androgens (male hormones)
  • Genetics and lifestyle factors
